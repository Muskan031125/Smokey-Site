Logging In & Your Role
Access
Roles
Permissions

Everything starts here. Log in with your email and password, and your role determines what you can see and do across the entire admin panel.

How to Log In

Open your browser and go to https://thejoharis.com/login

  • 1Enter your email address and password.
  • 2Click Sign In. You will land on the Dashboard.
  • 3To log out, click your name in the top-right corner and choose Log Out.
💡
First login? You may be prompted to set a new password. Use at least 8 characters. Always log out when finished, especially on shared computers.

Your Role

Every admin is assigned one of three roles. Your role controls what actions you can take.

⭐ Super Admin
Full access to everything — products, categories, clients, staff, settings, gold rate, bulk import, PDF export, home page, and client approvals.
📦 Inventory Manager
Can add and edit products, categories, bulk import, export PDFs, and view clients. Cannot manage staff or change system settings.
👁 Viewer
Read-only access. Can browse all inventory and the dashboard but cannot create, edit, or delete anything.
⚠️
Super Admin only: Approving client requests, managing staff accounts, and updating the gold rate all require Super Admin access.

Managing Products
Add SKUs
Edit Details
Upload Photos
Stock Status

Products are individual jewellery items in your catalogue. Each product has a unique Tag No and belongs to one collection.

Product List

Go to /admin/products to see all SKUs. Search by tag number, filter by collection, or sort by date.

ColumnWhat it shows
CoverThumbnail of the product's main photo.
Tag NoUnique product code, e.g. BR220, ER109. Click to view details.
CollectionThe jewellery category this product belongs to.
WeightGross weight and net (gold) weight in grams.
StockIn Stock or Out of Stock
Live TotalAuto-calculated price using today's gold rate.

Adding a New Product

Click + New SKU (top right). Fill in the form:

  • 1Enter the Tag No — must be unique across the entire catalogue.
  • 2Select a Category from the dropdown.
  • 3Enter gross weight, purity, net weight, and labour charge.
  • 4Add diamonds or stones if applicable (see below).
  • 5Tick In Stock if the item is available to clients.
  • 6Click Save SKU.

Weight & Purity Fields

FieldWhat to enter
Gross WeightTotal weight including all stones, in grams.
PurityAs a decimal — 0.75 for 18K, 0.916 for 22K, 0.99 for 24K.
Net WeightGold weight only (no stones), in grams. Used for price calculation.
LabourMaking/labour charge in ₹.
ColourYellow, White, Rose, or Two-Tone.
ℹ️
Price is calculated automatically:
Total = (Net Weight × Gold Rate × Purity) + Labour + Diamonds + Stones
You never need to enter a price manually — it updates in real time with the gold rate.

Diamonds & Stones

Diamonds: Click + Add Diamond for each group (up to 3). Enter number of pieces, total carat weight, and rate per carat. Optionally add shape, colour grade, and clarity grade.

Stones: Click + Add Stone for rubies, emeralds, etc. (up to 2). Enter name, weight, rate, and number of pieces.

Uploading Product Photos

After saving a product, click Manage Media to add photos.

  • 1Drag and drop images or click to browse. Select multiple at once. Formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP.
  • 2The first image uploaded becomes the cover photo automatically.
  • 3To change the cover, click the ⭐ icon on any image.
  • 4Drag images to reorder. Click 🗑 to delete.
  • 5To add a Vimeo video, paste the URL and click Add Video.

Toggling Stock Status

In the product list, click Toggle Stock to instantly switch a product between In Stock and Out of Stock. Out-of-stock items remain visible with a red badge.

Categories

Categories are the jewellery collections shown on the portal — Bracelets, Earrings, Rings, Pendants, etc. Every product belongs to exactly one category.

Creating a Category

Go to /admin/categories → click + New Category.

FieldWhat to enter
Name ✱The collection name as clients will see it. E.g. "Bracelet", "Ladies Ring". Required.
SlugAuto-filled from the name. No need to change unless you want a custom URL.
DescriptionOptional short description shown at the top of the collection page.
Sort OrderControls the display order on the portal. Lower = higher up. Use 10, 20, 30… for easy reordering.
Cover ImageUpload a photo or paste an image URL. Shown as the collection thumbnail on the home page.

Hiding a Category

Click Toggle in the category list to hide a collection from clients without deleting it. All products in a hidden category also become hidden. You can re-enable it at any time.

💡
Product count updates automatically. The number shown next to each category reflects the live count of products assigned to it.

Editing a Category

Click the category name to open the edit form. You can update the name, description, sort order, and cover image at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Bulk Import lets you add hundreds of SKUs at once from an Excel file, with product photos in a ZIP — instead of entering each product manually.

Go to /admin/bulk-import and select a mode:

📊
Excel + Photos
Upload an Excel file to create or update SKUs. Optionally include a ZIP of product photos.
🖼
Photos Only
Attach photos to products already in the system. No Excel needed — just the ZIP file.

Mode 1 — Excel + Photos

Step 1 — Download & fill the template

Click ↓ Download Template. Fill in one row per product. The template includes an Instructions sheet with full column details.

ColumnFieldNotes
ATag No ✱Unique product code. Required.
CGross WeightTotal weight in grams.
DPurity %Enter 75 for 18K, 91.6 for 22K, 99 for 24K.
ONet WeightGold weight only, in grams.
PLabourMaking charge in ₹.
AKIn Stock1 = In Stock, 0 = Out of Stock.
AMCategory ✱Must exactly match a category name in the system.
💡
Category auto-detection: Leave the Category column blank and the system will guess from the tag prefix — BR → Bracelet, ER → Earrings, LR → Ladies Ring. See the full prefix list in Section 12.

Step 2 — Prepare photos ZIP (optional)

Name each image after its Tag No and put them all in one ZIP:

FilenameResult
BR220.jpgCover photo for SKU BR220
BR220-002.jpgSecond photo for BR220
ER109.jpgCover photo for SKU ER109

Images can be inside sub-folders in the ZIP. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP.

Step 3 — Upload, preview & confirm

  • 1Upload the Excel file in zone 1. Upload the photos ZIP in zone 2 (optional).
  • 2Tick Override existing SKUs only if you want to update products already in the system.
  • 3Click Preview Data → and review the results table.
  • 4Click Confirm Import to complete.
✅
Automatic backup: The system saves a backup of your data before every import. You can restore from the Backups page if anything goes wrong.

Mode 2 — Photos Only

Select Photos Only from the dropdown. Upload your ZIP file (named by Tag No). Click Upload Photos. The system matches each image to the correct existing product automatically.

Backups & Restore

Click View Backups to see all pre-import snapshots with their date and size. Click Restore to roll back to a previous state.

⚠️
Restore overwrites current data. Only use this to undo a bulk import that produced incorrect results. This action cannot be undone.

Export as PDF
Catalogue PDF
Filter by Category
Share with Clients

Generate a professionally formatted PDF of your catalogue to share with clients by email or WhatsApp.

Go to /admin/export-pdf.

Export Options

OptionDescription
Select CollectionsChoose which categories to include. Leave all unticked to export the full catalogue.
Layout2 products per page (compact) or 1 product per page (detailed, with full specifications).
Stock FilterExport all items, in-stock only, or out-of-stock only.

Click Generate & Download PDF. The PDF includes product photos, all specifications, diamond and stone details, and the live calculated price at the current gold rate.

💡
Always update the gold rate first. Before sending a PDF to a client, check that Settings shows today's correct gold rate — prices in the PDF are calculated at the moment of generation.

Access Requests
Approve
Reject
Review History

When a new client submits an access request on the website, it appears here for review before they are granted access to the catalogue.

Go to /admin/access-requests. Each request displays the client's name, email, phone, company, business type, address, and GST number.

Approving a Request

  • 1Review the applicant's details carefully.
  • 2Click Approve.
  • 3The system automatically creates a login account for the client.
  • 4A welcome email with login credentials is sent to the client automatically.
  • 5The client will be asked to set a new password on their first login.

Rejecting a Request

Click Reject to decline. The request is logged with your name and timestamp. The applicant does not receive an automatic notification — contact them separately if needed.

ℹ️
The last 20 reviewed requests are shown below the pending list so you can refer back to recent decisions.

Managing Clients
View Accounts
Control Access
Activity Logs

View and manage all approved client accounts. Monitor what clients are browsing and control their access at any time.

Go to /admin/clients. The list shows each client's name, company, last active date, and status.

Client Actions

ActionWhat it does
Enable / DisableImmediately grants or removes catalogue access. The client sees a "not authorised" message when disabled.
Reset PasswordGenerates a new temporary password. Shown to you to share with the client. They must change it on next login.
Set Expiry DateRestrict access to a specific end date. After that date the client cannot log in.
Clear ActivityDeletes the client's browsing history. Their account stays active.

Activity Log

Click any client's name to open their profile. The activity log shows every page they have visited — which products and collections, when, and from which device. This helps you understand what your clients are most interested in.

Admin Staff
Create Users
Assign Roles
Manage Access

Manage all admin user accounts — create new staff, assign roles, reset passwords, and disable access when needed.

Go to /admin/staff. Only Super Admins can access this section.

Adding a New Staff Member

  • 1Click + New Internal User.
  • 2Enter their full name, email address, and a temporary password (minimum 8 characters).
  • 3Select their role: Super Admin, Inventory Manager, or Viewer.
  • 4Click Create User. They can now log in immediately.

Managing Existing Staff

ActionWhat it does
Change RoleSelect a new role and save. Takes effect immediately.
Reset PasswordGenerate a new temporary password. The staff member must change it on next login.
Enable / DisableRemove or restore admin access without deleting the account.
⚠️
Super Admin access is powerful. Only assign the Super Admin role to people who genuinely need full control — they can change settings, approve clients, and manage all data.

Settings & Gold Rate
Brand
Gold Rate
Regional

Configure your brand details, update the gold rate, and set regional preferences. Only Super Admins can access Settings.

Go to /admin/settings.

Brand Settings

SettingWhat it controls
Brand NameDisplayed in the portal header and in all emails sent to clients.
Contact EmailEmail address shown in welcome emails sent to new clients.
TaglineShort description shown on the portal home page.

Gold Rate Critical

The gold rate (₹ per gram, 24K) drives every product's live price. Update this regularly — ideally daily — to keep prices accurate for clients.

⚠️
All prices update the moment you save. There is no confirmation step. Double-check the value before clicking Save — an incorrect rate affects every product price instantly.

Regional Settings

SettingNotes
RegionIndia, US, EU, UK, UAE, or Singapore.
TimezoneAffects all timestamps throughout the admin panel.
CurrencyINR, USD, GBP, EUR, AED, or SGD. Symbol and decimals auto-fill.
Date / Time FormatChoose your preferred display style. A live preview is shown on the page.
